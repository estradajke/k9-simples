16 Control Flow

Though looping statements are not necessary in k9, if-then-else statements are sometimes useful. 

$[b;t;f] cond

16.1 cond ⇒ $[b;t;f]

If b is non zero then x else y. x and y are not required to be of the same type. 

 $[3>2;`a;`b]
`a

 $[2>3;`a;`b]
`b

 $[37;12;10]
12
